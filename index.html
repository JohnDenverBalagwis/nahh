About

Greetings,

We are delighted to introduce eTrack, a comprehensive web-based application tailored exclusively for the Office of the Vice President at Marinduque State College. Within its digital framework, eTrack offers a range of essential features, including Voting System , Accomplishment Reporting , Student Activity Monitoring , Activity Evaluation , and Management of Student Organization .

Our aim with eTrack is to simplify administrative processes, promote transparency, and empower our college community. We invite you to explore the possibilities and benefits that eTrack brings to Marinduque State College. Thank you for your interest in our platform, and we look forward to serving you. Welcome to a more efficient and vibrant campus life.

Warm regards,

Office of the Vice President for Student Affairs and Services
